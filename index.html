Abstract

This dataset represents a curated collection of high-resolution observations designed to benchmark state-of-the-art machine learning models. Collected over a period of 24 months, it encompasses diverse scenarios and edge cases essential for robust scientific inquiry.

Our methodology ensures high data integrity and provides detailed metadata for every entry, facilitating easy integration into existing research workflows.
